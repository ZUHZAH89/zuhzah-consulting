Our Terms & Privacy

Orders are confirmed upon receipt of payment or a confirmed payment arrangement. We accept mobile money (M-Pesa), bank transfer, and PayPal. Prices are as listed in our service packages.
Standard delivery is 2 business days from order confirmation. Express (48 hrs) and urgent (24 hrs) delivery are available for applicable packages. Timelines begin once all required client information has been received.
Revision requests must be submitted within 3 days of document delivery. Revisions are based on the original brief; significant scope changes may incur additional charges. Refunds are considered on a case-by-case basis if the delivered document does not meet the agreed brief after all revision rounds have been exhausted. Refunds are not issued for change of mind after work has commenced.
All personal information and documents shared with Zuhzah Consulting are treated with strict confidentiality. We do not sell, share, or disclose your information to third parties except as required by law. Documents and personal data are stored securely and deleted upon your request. You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time.
All documents produced by Zuhzah Consulting become the property of the client upon full payment. These terms are governed by the laws of the Republic of Kenya. Any disputes shall be resolved through mutual negotiation or, where necessary, through the appropriate Kenyan legal channels.
